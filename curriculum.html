Detailed Module Breakdown

Each module builds upon the previous, creating a comprehensive learning journey from strategic leadership to advanced AI implementation.

Module 1

Strategic Marketing Analytics Leadership

Week 1 (4 hours) Leadership Focus

Transform from tactical reporting to strategic leadership. Learn to build high-performing analytics teams, influence C-suite decision making, and design technology strategies that drive business growth.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the evolving role of marketing analytics in Fortune 500 companies
  • Develop frameworks for building high-performing analytics teams
  • Create strategic roadmaps for marketing technology integration
  • Build organizational influence and executive communication skills

Key Topics

From Reporting to Strategic Partnership
Team Building & Organizational Design
Technology Stack Strategy
Executive Communication

Practical Deliverables

  • Organizational structure design for analytics team
  • Technology ROI analysis and vendor evaluation
  • 90-day quick wins implementation plan
  • Executive presentation on analytics transformation
Module 2

Advanced Attribution & Measurement

Weeks 2-3 (8 hours) Technical Deep-Dive

Master sophisticated attribution methodologies that work in the privacy-first era. Implement multi-touch attribution, marketing mix modeling, and incrementality testing at enterprise scale.

Learning Outcomes

  • Implement sophisticated multi-touch attribution models
  • Design and validate marketing mix models for enterprise budgets
  • Execute incrementality testing frameworks
  • Navigate measurement challenges in privacy-first world

Key Topics

Multi-Touch Attribution Models
Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)
Incrementality & Experimentation
Privacy-First Measurement

Practical Deliverables

  • Attribution model implementation (Python/R)
  • Marketing mix model for CPG brand case study
  • Incrementality testing framework design
  • Privacy-compliant measurement strategy

Customer Analytics & Segmentation

Develop advanced customer segmentation strategies and predictive analytics. Build customer lifetime value models and create personalization frameworks that scale across millions of customers.

  • Implement churn prediction and prevention systems

Key Topics

Advanced Segmentation Techniques
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Modeling

Enterprise Personalization Strategy

Practical Deliverables

Budget Allocation & Optimization

Prerequisites & Requirements

This advanced program is designed for experienced marketing analytics professionals ready to take on executive leadership roles.

Required Experience

  • 5+ years marketing analytics experience
  • Advanced Excel/Google Sheets proficiency
  • Basic SQL query writing ability
  • Experience with analytics platforms (GA, Adobe Analytics, etc.)
  • Team leadership experience (3+ people)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Python or R programming exposure
  • Statistical analysis experience
  • Marketing automation platform experience
  • Data visualization tool experience (Tableau, Power BI)
  • MBA or quantitative graduate degree

Technical Requirements

Modern laptop/desktop with 8GB+ RAM
Stable internet connection (25+ Mbps)
Webcam and microphone for live sessions
Access to cloud platforms (provided)

All software licenses and cloud access are provided as part of the course fee.
