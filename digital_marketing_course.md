# Advanced Digital Marketing Analytics Course
## For Fortune 500 Marketing Directors

### Course Overview

**Target Audience**: Director of Marketing Analytics at Fortune 500 Companies  
**Duration**: 12 weeks (3-4 hours per week)  
**Format**: Hybrid (Online modules + Live workshops + Case studies)  
**Prerequisites**: 5+ years marketing analytics experience, advanced Excel/SQL skills

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:
- Design and implement enterprise-scale marketing measurement frameworks
- Lead cross-functional teams in data-driven marketing transformation
- Build predictive models for customer lifetime value and attribution
- Present actionable insights to C-suite executives
- Navigate privacy regulations and cookieless future strategies
- Optimize multi-million dollar marketing budgets using advanced analytics

---

## Module 1: Strategic Marketing Analytics Leadership (Week 1)

### Learning Outcomes
- Understand the evolving role of marketing analytics in Fortune 500 companies
- Develop frameworks for building high-performing analytics teams
- Create strategic roadmaps for marketing technology integration

### Content
1. **The Modern Marketing Analytics Leader**
   - From reporting to strategic partnership
   - Building influence across the organization
   - Managing up: Communicating with CMOs and CEOs

2. **Team Building & Organizational Design**
   - Structuring analytics teams for scale
   - Hiring strategies for specialized roles
   - Creating data-driven culture initiatives

3. **Technology Stack Strategy**
   - Evaluating enterprise marketing technology platforms
   - Integration architecture planning
   - ROI frameworks for martech investments

### Practical Exercise
Design an organizational structure and 12-month roadmap for a marketing analytics team supporting a $500M revenue business

---

## Module 2: Advanced Attribution & Measurement (Week 2-3)

### Learning Outcomes
- Implement sophisticated multi-touch attribution models
- Design incrementality testing frameworks
- Navigate measurement challenges in privacy-first world

### Content
1. **Beyond Last-Click Attribution**
   - Algorithmic attribution modeling
   - Time-decay and position-based models
   - Custom attribution for complex B2B journeys

2. **Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)**
   - Statistical foundations of MMM
   - Building and validating MMM for enterprise scale
   - Integrating MMM with attribution data

3. **Incrementality & Experimentation**
   - Geo-holdout testing design
   - Synthetic control methods
   - Causal inference in marketing

4. **Privacy-First Measurement**
   - Cookieless attribution strategies
   - First-party data activation
   - Privacy-preserving analytics techniques

### Case Study
Redesign attribution strategy for a Fortune 500 retailer transitioning from third-party cookies

---

## Module 3: Customer Analytics & Segmentation (Week 4-5)

### Learning Outcomes
- Develop advanced customer segmentation strategies
- Build predictive customer lifetime value models
- Create personalization frameworks at enterprise scale

### Content
1. **Advanced Segmentation Techniques**
   - Behavioral clustering using machine learning
   - Psychographic segmentation for B2B
   - Dynamic segmentation for real-time personalization

2. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Modeling**
   - Probabilistic CLV models (Buy-Till-You-Die)
   - Machine learning approaches to CLV
   - Integrating CLV into marketing strategy

3. **Predictive Analytics for Marketing**
   - Churn prediction and prevention
   - Next-best-action recommendation engines
   - Propensity scoring for campaign optimization

4. **Enterprise Personalization Strategy**
   - Personalization maturity frameworks
   - Real-time decisioning architecture
   - Measuring personalization impact

### Workshop
Build a CLV model using real anonymized Fortune 500 customer data
